We are extremely cat friendly! You can bring your own cat as a guest with you with no extra charge.

Our rooms are equipped with cat food, food bowls, litter boxes and toys to properly care for your felines needs. Room service comes in to clean the litter box upon request.

COVID Guidelines

  • Human guests are required to wear a mask in the lobby and all common areas except for the Anna’s Cat Cafe and the pool.

  • Employees are required to wear a mask at all times.

  • No more than 10 people are to be in the pool area at a time. Please remain 6 feet away from non-family members.

  • Cats do not have to wear masks because COVID-19 is not cool enough to infect them.
